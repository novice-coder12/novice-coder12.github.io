Dgplug Summer Training 2014

What is Dgplug?

Well,Dgplug is a Linux's user group of Durgapur.It is a group of some open source enthusiasts who have been organizing this summer training since past seven years.This training has brought together people from all corners of the world, who share the same passion for learning something new.From a novice coder (like me) to some amazing developers who are working in big techies are there, contributing and sharing experiences with us. I am looking forward to connect with this awesome bunch of people and contribute.

When? Where? What?

This training is completely free.All that you need is a computer with a GNU/Linux distribution installed,an internet connection and yes! an attitude to learn and stay passionate about it.The training began from 29th June 2014 and is still in process.The classes are held regularly at an IRC channel.Guest lectures are also arranged as an incentives.The objective of this training includes:

  • Revisiting programming fundamentals
  • Get acquainted with Free Software technologies
  • Gaining technical knowledge
  • Real-world project experience

Authors and Contributors

So,who is the person behind the idea? Well,I must mention that this initiative is taken up by Kushal Das(@kushaldas),a senior software engineer at RedHat and he is supported by his students Sayan Chowdhury(@sayanchowdhury) and Chandan Kumar and some others.These people have contributed a lot to this training by sharing their experiences and knowledge.

How to join?

Sounds cool,enough? You also want to join? Yes, you can.You just need to fill this form and join classes at IRC channel #dgplug.And for any information,you can always contact me(@novice-coder12) or send a query by subscribing at the mailing list.
